GoCapture Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 17, 2015



Welcome to GoCapture! Where with one simple click you can send your captured moment to the world! We offer a fun and insightful way to show what’s going on in the world around you at the touch of your fingertips! But before we start, we have to talk a bit about your privacy rights to ensure you understand what information we collect, how we use and disclose that information and what your responsibilities are when using the GoCapture Services.


First of all, we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality and security of your personal information. It’s just that simple. If you have any issues contact us and we’ll do our best to make it right!


We will use all reasonable efforts to protect your personal information but if you do not want us to have access to your personal information, please do not provide it to us. By using or accessing our Services (as defined in our Terms of Service ), or otherwise providing us with your personal information, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information in accordance with this privacy policy.


We collection the following types of information:


  1. Information You Provide – this is all information you voluntarily provide to us, such as your name, username, and address;


  1. Information from Facebook – to use the Services you must login with your Facebook account. By doing so, your provide us with information from your Facebook account in a manner consistent with your Facebook privacy settings;


  1. Location/Device Information – to use the Services you must also provide us with your location information from your mobile device;


  1. Digital Media Content – when using the Services, you may provide us with photos or videos and captions in relation to such photos or videos;


  1. Cookies – cookies are small text files that may be placed on your device that provide certain information to us about how you use the Services;


  1. Information Collected Automatically – we may collect certain information from your device when you use the Services such as your IP address, device ID and type, your browser type and language, and access times, and


  1. User Communications – when you send email or other correspondence to us we may retain those communications. If you communicate with another user of the Services, you also allow provide us with the content of those conversations.

How You Use That Information:


The Services are designed to allow you to post pictures and/or videos, and information about those pictures and/or videos, to a feed that is accessible to the public. You also have the option of making your posts private although this may limit the features available in the Services. Your posts are paired with your location information, as set by you in the privacy settings. The privacy settings in the Services give you the ability to control how the Services display some your personal information, including information displayed in your profile, who can access your profile and posted content, and how your location is displayed. How you choose to use the Services is your responsibility. By default, all privacy settings in the Services are made public in order to provide you with the best experience when using the Services.


You have the right to access your personal information held by us (subject to limited lawful exceptions) and to request that such information be corrected if it is inaccurate or incomplete. On request, you may view and edit the personal information we have on file about you, or request that we delete it. To make a request, please contact us. In some situations we may not be able to satisfy a request (for example, where this would jeopardize the privacy of others).
